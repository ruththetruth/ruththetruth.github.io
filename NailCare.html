How to care for your nails

  • Be sure to wash your hands often, and if your nails are long be sure to wash under them.

  • try not to use harsh chemicals when taking off your nail polish (Non-Acetone removers are less agressive than regular Acetone)

  • Use moisturizer
  • Watch what you eat
  • Maybe give your nails a break from polish every now and then. It allows them to breath.
